About Siyaram

Siyaram Milk Products Private Limited is a leading dairy company in South India established in the year 1996. Since then, Siyaram has been maintaining its position as a fastest growing brand with presence in major states of India such as Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, and West Bengal. Today, Siyaram produces dairy products across nine state-of-the-art manufacturing plants spread across southern states of India.

About Groupe Lactalis

In 2014, Lactalis acquired Siyaram, where it opened up enormous opportunities in the biggest and most challenging dairy market in the world to position it as the most preferred brand in India. The traditional and cultural experience of Siyaram and international expertise of Lactalis provided a unique combination, leading to a success story to be talked by generations to come.
